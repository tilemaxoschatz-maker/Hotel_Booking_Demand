# Hotel Booking Demand Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking demand data from a Portuguese hotel company. The objective is to understand customer booking behavior, identify the main drivers of booking cancellations, and provide business recommendations to improve hotel performance.

The analysis focuses on customer characteristics, booking patterns, seasonality, hotel types, and cancellation behavior.

---

## Business Objectives

The main goals of this project are:

- Analyze hotel booking demand
- Investigate booking cancellations
- Identify customer segments with higher cancellation rates
- Understand seasonal booking trends
- Evaluate the impact of lead time, ADR, and special requests
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## Dataset

Source: Kaggle – Hotel Booking Demand Dataset

The dataset contains hotel booking information for both City Hotels and Resort Hotels.

After data cleaning:

- 87,396 observations
- 32 variables

---

## Data Cleaning

The following steps were performed:

- Checked for missing values
- Handled missing values in key variables
- Removed duplicate records
- Verified data quality before analysis

---

## Business Questions

1. What percentage of hotel bookings are canceled?
2. Which hotel type experiences more cancellations?
3. Does lead time affect booking cancellations?
4. Which customer types cancel most frequently?
5. Which countries generate the most bookings?
6. Which months have the highest booking demand?
7. Does Average Daily Rate (ADR) influence cancellations?
8. Which market segments generate the most bookings?
9. Which distribution channels are most effective?
10. Does the number of special requests affect cancellations?

---

## Key Findings

- Approximately 37% of bookings are canceled.
- City Hotels experience significantly more cancellations than Resort Hotels.
- Customers who book further in advance are more likely to cancel.
- Transient customers have the highest cancellation rates.
- Portugal generates the highest number of bookings.
- Summer months show the strongest booking demand.
- ADR has limited influence on cancellations.
- Online Travel Agencies generate the majority of bookings.
- Customers with more special requests are significantly less likely to cancel.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Implement stricter cancellation policies for long lead-time bookings.
- Develop retention strategies for transient customers.
- Strengthen partnerships with high-performing booking channels.
- Introduce loyalty incentives to reduce cancellations.
- Encourage customer engagement through personalized services and special requests.
